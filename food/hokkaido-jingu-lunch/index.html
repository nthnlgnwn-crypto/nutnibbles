Sapporo / 20 Jan 2025 / Lunch

A comforting lunch near Hokkaido Jingu.

After visiting the shrine, lunch nearby was simple and filling: omurice, fried seafood, salad, and a hamburger-style patty with sauce.

Personal Review

Easy, satisfying, and just right between stops

This was the kind of lunch that works well during a travel day. Nothing too heavy or complicated, but warm, familiar, and filling enough before continuing to the next place.

The plate had a little bit of everything, which made it feel like a comfortable family-trip lunch rather than a destination meal.

Worth it? Yes, as a practical lunch stop near the shrine.
