United Kingdom / 7-22 Nov 2023

London first, then a slow climb into Scotland.

This is the UK and Scotland trip, prepared from the photo trail: London streets and comfort food, Windsor stone walls, Manchester and Blackpool stops, then a colder, moodier stretch through Glasgow, the Highlands, Inverness, Stirling, and Edinburgh.

Route

London to Edinburgh, with a northern middle.

The trip begins in London, moves through Windsor and Manchester, follows the road toward Scotland, and ends with Edinburgh lights before returning south.

Vibe

Autumn streets, warm plates, grey skies.

Less checklist travel, more little scenes: a lake in London, red phone boxes, rainy paths, road views, whisky rooms, city lights, and food that kept the cold manageable.

London

First city rhythm

Architecture, parks, market bites, and easy comfort-food stops before the route moved north.

England stops

Castles, meals, and small detours

Windsor brought the castle mood, while Manchester and Blackpool added casual meals and lighter city moments.

Scotland

The road got moodier

Autumn paths, grey Highland roads, whisky rooms, and stone monuments gave this chapter a heavier, colder atmosphere.

Edinburgh

Old stone and winter lights

Edinburgh closed the trip with gothic buildings, palace lawns, night lights, and one final return through London.
